# Rocktron MIDI Rack Units

## Overview

Rocktron offers a range of **1U rackmount units** designed for guitarists seeking high-quality tone shaping, effects, and noise reduction.  These units are known for their **DSP-powered processing**, **tube emulation**, and **studio/live performance versatility**.

### Rocktron Voodu Valve

A **tube/DSP hybrid preamp** featuring a **12AX7 tube** for warm, dynamic response. Combines tube drive with 24-bit digital effects (reverb, delay, chorus, etc.) and advanced **speaker simulation**. Ideal for direct recording or PA use. Still in production with a limited-edition re-release available.

###### Rocktron Prophesy II

A powerful **4-channel DSP preamp** with distinct voicings: **Clean American**, **Texas Blues**, **Vintage British**, and **Mega Drive** (high-gain).  Features **Dynamic Tube Replication (DTR)**, parametric EQ, **Pluck Detection** for dynamic effect control, and a programmable speaker simulator.  Favored by players like Dave Mustaine and Rusty Cooley.

### Rocktron Chameleon

A highly programmable **digital preamp** from the 1990s, praised for its tonal flexibility—especially in **high-gain and progressive metal** styles.  Known for crystal-clear cleans and aggressive distortion. The **Chameleon Online** version added web-based patch downloads.

### Rocktron Piranha

A **dual 12AX7 tube preamp** delivering high-gain tones comparable to a **Mesa Dual Rectifier**.  Features a **sweepable mid frequency** for precise tone shaping, robust clean channel, and 99 presets.  Regarded as one of Rocktron’s best all-tube rack units.

### Rocktron Xpression

A **multi-effects processor** (not a preamp) offering up to **10 simultaneous effects**, including reverb, delay, pitch, and modulation. Includes **speaker and mic modeling**, and a unique analog dry path option. Designed to pair with external preamps. 

### Rocktron HUSH Series

Noise reduction units like the **HUSH Super C**, **HUSH Pro**, and **HUSH IICX**, are professional-grade **noise reduction system**s designed for guitarists, known for eliminating hiss, hum, and feedback without compromising tone.  It uses a combination of **dynamic filtering** and **downward expansion** (VCA-based) to reduce noise transparently, preserving sustain and note decay.

## Rocktron Replifex

The **Rocktron Replifex** is a **24-bit rackmount multi-effects processor** from the mid-90s, designed for guitarists seeking studio-quality effects with live performance flexibility.  It features a comprehensive suite of **high-quality stereo effects**, including reverb, delay, chorus, flanger, phaser, pitch shift, tremolo, auto-pan, rotary speaker simulation, and a **HUSH® noise reduction system**.

### Other non-MIDI Models

- **Gainiac**: Entry-level preamp, limited features

- ***Widowmaker**: High-gain preamp with tube drive
