Here are four of my favourite patches :

Bronto-Stomp
The name says it all: simple, dirty, aggressive, BIG!!! (I don't know about
Brontos being aggressive, but you get the picture...)
Nice for Core, Rage against T.M.-kinda stuff.
Play it totally dry for even more attack.

MXR Phase90 (TM)
My rip-off of a pedal my roommate bought, it's NOT the real thing, so don't
blame me if you don't think the title is inaccurate, it's more my
impression of it, and remember: you love your guitar, so give these strings
a nice rub!

Box o'Crunch
No High-Gain here!
Good old wannabe a Superlead-dirt.
(Even the tubefanatics like this one.)

Bass-Mares
Finally, the sound to scare your neighbors enough so they wont bother you
anymore while you're practicing. Good if your bass-player doesnt show, or
make a trio situation sound BIG!
