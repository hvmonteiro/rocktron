**Rocktron Chameleon** is a highly regarded 24-bit DSP guitar preamp and multi-effects processor that has been a staple in professional studio and live rigs since its introduction in the 1990s.  Known for its **exceptional tone quality**, **comprehensive effects suite**, and **advanced speaker simulation**, it stands out as a versatile tool for guitarists across genres—from clean country to heavy metal. 

### Key Features:

- **Four Distinct Channels**: Clean, Texas, British, and Mega—each modeled after iconic amp tones (e.g., Tweed, Plexi) with **Dynamic Tube Replication (DTR)** for authentic tube-like warmth. 

- **Full Parametric EQ**: Includes **pre-distortion** and **post-distortion 2-band and 4-band parametric EQs**, allowing precise shaping of tone before and after gain stages. 

- **Professional-Grade Effects**: Built-in **chorus, phaser, flanger, delay (1 sec with tap tempo), reverb, tremolo, pitch shift, compressor, and wah**, all known for their "retro analog" character and clarity. 

- **Advanced Speaker Simulation**: Offers **speaker size (8", 10", 12", 15", full range), mic position, and reactance controls**—delivering realistic cabinet tone at line level for direct recording or P.A. use. 

- **HUSH Noise Reduction**: Eliminates high-gain noise without sacrificing sustain or tone. 

- **MIDI & Remote Control**: Full MIDI implementation with support for Rocktron MIDI foot controllers (e.g., All Access, MIDI Mate) and **8-parameter continuous control**. 

- **Stereo XLR Outputs**: Ideal for direct-to-mixer or stereo P.A. applications. 

### Presets & Community:

- **Downloadable Presets**: Rocktron offers a vast library of user-created and factory presets via the **[Patch Bay](https://www.rocktronusa.com/patchbay---chameleon.html)**, including:
  
  - **Def Leppard** and **Southern Rock** tones
  
  - **U2-style shimmer** effects
  
  - **Heavy metal lead patches**
  
  - **Yngwie Malmsteen** emulations
  
  - **Custom patches from professionals** like **Gary Hoey** and **Don Petersen**

- **Chameleon On-Line** and **Chameleon 2000** models support **bulk loading** of up to 254 presets (original and online collections). 

### User Experience & Value:

- Praised for its **transparent, high-fidelity sound** and **unmatched flexibility**, the Chameleon is often described as a **"Swiss Army knife"** for tone shaping. 

- While it lacks an FX loop or preamp bypass (a noted limitation), its **value for money** remains exceptional—especially in the used market. 

- Still used by pros like **J. of White Zombie**, **Gary Hoey**, and **Nine Inch Nails** in live and studio settings. 

### Legacy & Modern Relevance:

- Despite being designed in the '90s, the Chameleon remains **highly sought after** due to its **superior algorithms and build quality**. 

- It’s considered one of the most affordable high-end options in a market dominated by Eventide, TC, and Lexicon.

- Rocktron continues to support it with **PC-based editors** (e.g., **Chameleon 2000 Sysex Editor**) and **MIDI utilities** like MIDI-OX. 

For guitarists seeking a **powerful, programmable, and studio-quality preamp with effects**, the Rocktron Chameleon is a timeless, reliable choice—especially when paired with a tube amp or used directly into a mixer.
