this is a preset for the Voodu Valve which was originally 
designed to reproduce Gary Moore's 80's sound. You know, the time
when he was still heavily rocking and dive-bombing with Jackson 
and Hamer guitars.

This sound is really spacey to give it the richness needed for his
soloworks at the time, especially with songs like "The Loner" and 
"Empty Rooms" in mind. It hardly has bottom-end but lots of mids and
presence and thus stands out nicely against the bass and drums.

I hope you enjoy this sound
Guido Henkel
