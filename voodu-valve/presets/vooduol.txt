This file contains all 127 VOODU VALVE ON-LINE presets.

This file is a BULK DUMP of the VooDu Valve On-Line presets, so it
must be loaded into a VooDu Valve as a BULK LOAD.  These presets 
may be loaded into ANY version of the VooDu Valve. 

The VooDu Valve must receive the data at the same rate that it sends.
If you encounter a "RECEIVE ERROR" when performing a bulk load, then
you'll need to slow down the send rate.

To adjust Dumpsters send rate, click on Options, then MIDI Devices.
At the bottom of the window will be a parameter called "Delay between
blocks".  Increase the delay time until you no longer experience a
receive error.

This will overwrite all your current presets so it is suggested that
you use Dumpster to back up your current presets before loading these
new On-Line presets.  If you back up your own presets individually 
(not bulk), then you may load back your favorite custom presets in the 
preset locations you so choose, allowing you to keep the new On-Line 
presets of your choice.
