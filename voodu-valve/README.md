## Rocktron Voodu Valve: DSP Tube Guitar Preamp Overview

The **Rocktron Voodu Valve** is a professional rackmount guitar preamp that combines **tube warmth** with **24-bit digital signal processing (DSP)** for high-fidelity tone shaping and effects.  It features a **12AX7EH vacuum tube** in the preamp stage, delivering the rich, dynamic response of classic tube amps in a compact 1U rack format. 

Designed for both **studio recording** and **live performance**, the Voodu Valve integrates advanced speaker simulation, allowing direct output to mixers, audio interfaces, or PA systems without needing a physical guitar cabinet.  It includes **254 factory presets**, with additional user-programmable options and downloadable patches via Rocktron’s Patch Bay. 



## Key Features

### 1. **Tube-Driven Preamp with Voicing Options**

The preamp section offers selectable **high or low tube gain** for varying levels of natural tube breakup.  It includes multiple voicing modes:

- **Variac**: Simulates voltage sag for vintage amp feel

- **Pentode/Triode**: Adjusts power amp character

- **Solid State**: Clean, modern response 

This allows guitarists to dial in tones ranging from **sparkling cleans** to **high-gain saturation**. 

### 2. **Digital Effects Suite**

The Voodu Valve includes a comprehensive set of **studio-quality digital effects**, modeled after Rocktron’s acclaimed stompboxes:

- Reverb

- Delay (with tap tempo)

- Chorus (2-tap)

- Flanger (2-tap)

- Phaser (dual-mode)

- Tremolo

- Pitch shifting

- Compression

- Frequency-programmable wah-wah

These effects are placed post-preamp for natural integration with the tube tone. 

### 3. **Advanced Tone Shaping & Noise Control**

- **Full parametric EQ**: 4-band post-EQ and comprehensive pre-EQ for precise tone sculpting

- **HUSH Noise Reduction**: Active noise gating that eliminates hum and hiss during silence, placed between preamp and effects for optimal performance

- **Internal Wah-Wah**: Can be controlled via expression pedal without external hardware 

### 4. **Speaker Simulation & Direct Recording**

The **Advanced Speaker Simulation** is a standout feature, offering control over:

- Speaker size (15", 12", 8", full range)

- Mic placement (on-axis to edge)

- **Reactance parameter**: Adds cabinet resonance and "thump" for realistic miked cab emulation 

This makes the unit ideal for **silent practice**, **DI recording**, and **front-of-house direct input**. 

## Connectivity & Control

- **Inputs**: 1/4" mono unbalanced

- **Outputs**:
  
  - Dual 1/4" unbalanced (left/right)
  
  - Dual XLR balanced (with level control)
  
  - Headphone output (via left 1/4" jack, supports 600+ ohm headphones)

- **MIDI**: 7-pin DIN In, 5-pin DIN Thru/Out for full MIDI control

- **Footcontroller Support**: Compatible with Rocktron All Access, MIDI Mate, or MIDI Xchange for preset changes, effect toggling, and boost functions
